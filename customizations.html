Customizations

While there are multitudes of setting variables and definitions in any drawing environment, IntuiCAD has attempted to consolidate them into easily managed groupings for customization purposes.

 

For example, drawing setup routines pull information directly from layer manipulator routines. Change the layers and it is reflected in the drawing setup.

 

Blocks have been created under strict parameters and procedures to allow maximum user editing options. Liberal use of Autocad’s special tools; such as the name standard, the layers 0 and defpoints and the properties byblock and bylayer allow greater flexibility for individual offices as they adapt their libraries for new uses.

 

IntuiCAD will even track your customizations for upgrading purposes (see our Privacy Policy). We also provide substantial discounts for group customizations. Some of our other services include:

 

  1. CAD system analysis.
  2. CAD administration.
  3. Project management.
  4. Administrative training.
  5. CAD user training.
  6. System documentation.
  7. Remote monitoring and support.
  8. Library management and integrity.
