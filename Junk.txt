Like our products, IntuiCAD, LLC, grew naturally from necessity and experience.

One of the last generation of pre-CAD drafters, the developer of IntuiCAD SCM was rigidly drilled in visualizing and sketching techniques.  Great value was placed on the ability to quickly convey ideas and conceptions during the design process and keep the projects in fluid progression.

As the CAD invasion swelled during the 1990's and the software became more adaptable, abilities to visualize and sketch were supplanted by the abilities to navigate minefields of settings and ever changing dialogue boxes.  CAD moved design drafting in a decidedly mechanized direction.  AutoLisp programming alleviated some of the monotony but programming was largely unfunded and effective routines highly guarded.

It was at this time that several simultaneous high profile projects (and a bit of luck) necessitated a comprehensive package of structural design tools that could be accessed by project instead of by office.  Thus began the practice of separating CAD standards from the CAD tools themselves.
  
Once finally unburdened of "visual complexity" and with a boost from AutoCad's powerful dynamic blocks, the system has exploded into an arsenal of highly efficient and adaptable tools dedicated exclusively to time management. 

Now you can have the same intimate CAD customization we have provided to the A/E design community for over fifteen years. Take advantage of our massive database of mark-ups and input.  Get ALL the tools you may ever need for the price of the ones you just can't be without.
