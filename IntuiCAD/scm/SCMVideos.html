SCM Videos

This is a graveyard of sorts for all the different SCM videos that didn’t make the front pages but are either informative in some manner or just fun (preferably both). If any users have an idea for a video or have one of their own to share, please contact us.
 

Steel joists specifically tuned for detail adjustments. Note the coordinated insertion points that allow fluid and sequential motion.

 

This video runs through five different setup routines, preparing layout/model, model, revision, quick and

tilt-panel shop drawing setup routines.

 

 

The plan ladder comes with views for step-thru and caged conditions. It even has an adjustable side-step

visibility, with and without platform framing members.

 

Step-thru ladders come with variable rail members. The front and side elevations are coordinated to allow

adjustments in one view to reflect in all views.

 

The SCM Steel Mill is a parametric steel shapes routine that creates pline objects from database files. Metric

sizes, plan and elevation views and miscellaneous shapes are included.

 

A closer look at the stair tread block reveals the stringer ends prepared for connection and the intermediate

platform details.
